# hk-daily-newletter
HK Company Daily Newsletter Auto-Publisher


## Short Description
A fully automated open-source workflow that scrapes, compiles and publishesHong Kong corporate daily newsletters unattended. It auto-collects HK company updates, regulatory changes, industry trends and listed firm news, and outputs standardized daily briefings in classic newsletter format.
Core Highlights
An automated open-source tool designed to generate and publish daily newsletters focused on Hong Kong company updates, corporate affairs, industry trends and regulatory news. Inspired by the classic hk-daily-newsletter format, this project eliminates manual content sorting and publishing work, enabling fully unattended daily delivery of structured Hong Kong corporate briefings.
- Auto Scheduled Release: Fully unattended daily generation and publishing of HK corporate newsletters via scheduled tasks.
- HK Business Focused: Aggregates company registration updates, compliance policies, tax & legal regulations, listed company announcements and industry dynamics.
- Standard & Clean Layout: Follows mature hk-daily-newsletter structure with categorized and concise content.
- Fully Configurable: Customizable content sources, filtering rules, templates and publishing channels for secondary development.
- Lightweight Deployment: Low dependency, easy setup, compatible with most servers and automated operation platforms.
- 24/7 Automatic Daily Publishing: Runs scheduled tasks automatically every day to compile, generate and push the latest Hong Kong company newsletters without manual intervention, ensuring stable and continuous content output.
- Focused HK Corporate Content: Covers core Hong Kong business scenarios, including company registration updates, corporate compliance policies, industry market dynamics, listed company announcements, tax and legal regulatory changes, and local enterprise development trends.
- Standard Newsletter Structure: Adopts a clean, unified hk-daily-newsletter-style layout with categorized content, concise summaries and clear logic, meeting daily reading, business research and information collation needs.
- Highly Customizable: Supports flexible configuration of content sources, publishing frequency, newsletter templates and output formats. Developers can expand monitoring fields, adjust content filtering rules and customize publishing channels according to actual demands.
- Lightweight & Easy Deployment: Built with lightweight code architecture, low dependency and simple deployment process. It is compatible with mainstream server and automated operation platforms, suitable for personal use, team business inquiry and industry information aggregation.
